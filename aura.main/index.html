What is Aura Gold Water

Aura Gold Water is an ultra-premium water bottled at source in the pollution free wilderness of Banat Mountains in Romania.

Clean yet distinct taste and low mineral content, AUR’A is recognized as one of the purest bottled waters available with very low TDS. The unique yet classic cylindrical glass bottle is designed to complement the setting of your property.

Available in Still in 100% recyclable glass bottles.

One of the most high-end, exclusive waters on Earth

Although there’s plenty of water brands available on the market, just a few are able to truly compete with AUR’A.

The lab analysis performed by the renowned Wessling Lab in Germany and the National Institute of Molecular Technologies highlight the exceptional chemical and microbiological parameters which cannot be compared to any of the mineral waters available on the Market.
